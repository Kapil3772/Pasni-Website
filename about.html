About Pasni Rental

Preserving Traditions, Creating Memories

Our Story

Pasni Rental was born from a deep respect for Nepali traditions and a desire to make these sacred ceremonies accessible to every family. Founded with the vision of preserving our cultural heritage, we have been serving families across Nepal for years, helping them celebrate the important milestone of their child's first rice feeding ceremony.

Our journey began when we realized that many families struggled to gather all the traditional items needed for a proper Pasni ceremony. We decided to bridge this gap by offering comprehensive rental packages that include everything from ceremonial utensils to traditional outfits, ensuring that no family has to compromise on tradition due to accessibility or budget constraints.

Today, we take pride in being Nepal's trusted partner for traditional ceremonies, combining authentic cultural elements with modern convenience to create unforgettable experiences for families.

Our Values

Cultural Authenticity

We ensure every item in our collection respects traditional practices and maintains the sacred nature of the Pasni ceremony.

Quality Excellence

From silver utensils to ceremonial cloths, we maintain the highest standards of quality in all our rental items.

Family-Centered Service

We understand that every family is unique, and we work closely with you to customize the ceremony to your preferences.

Affordable Tradition

We believe that cultural traditions should be accessible to all families, regardless of their budget constraints.
